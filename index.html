Welcome to the D3finte Satellite Explorer! We have nearly 6000 different satellites for you to learn about. Feel free to filter them by their country of origin, orbit type, and more using the table drop downs. Use the sliders to choose how many satellites are displayed, or limit the latest launch year. Want to compare two differents satellites? Simply click on them on the table, and further details will be shown below. You can see trends in your satellite selection by consulting the chart to the bottom right. Want to start over? Simply click the earth and start fresh.

Tip: Click on any of the column headers to sort!
